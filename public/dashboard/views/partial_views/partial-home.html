How can I set my profile picture?

In this application are used Gravatar. A Gravatar is an image that follows you from site to site and will appear next to your name being associated to your email address.
To change your profile picture, sign up on Gravatar.com (link) using the email address you use to log on to this platform, or, if you are already registered, you can simply change your Gravatar image at this link.
